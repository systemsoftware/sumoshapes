# Sumo Shapes
Dive into the chaotic world of Sumo Shapes, where quick reflexes and strategic maneuvers are your keys to victory! In this exhilarating 2D fighting game, you and your friends transform into dynamic geometric characters in fast-paced battles on an ever-changing stage!

## Key Features:

### Local Multiplayer
Gather your friends and face off in thrilling matches where only the best shape will reign supreme. Perfect for parties and friendly competitions.
### Intuitive Phone Controls
Seamlessly connect your smartphone via your Wi-Fi to the game and use it as your controller. Experience a new level of interaction and precision as you navigate your character on the big screen.
### Diverse Shapes
Choose from an array of colorful geometric characters to fight with.
### Dynamic Stage
Battle across an ever-changing arena, with random platforms and set the background to any image or color!
### Build Your Own Stage
Battle across a stage of your own design or find one that someone else made!

---
Note: Ensure that both your phone and computer are connected to the same Wi-Fi network for seamless gameplay and to avoid any connectivity issues. If you cannot move, reload the controller page.
